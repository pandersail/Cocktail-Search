How our Search Works:

Welcome to Cocktail Mix, here you can find a whole world of cocktail recipes. There are three ways to search:

  1. By Name: Know exactly what you're looking for, or only remember on word from the name? Great! Put in the name or word, and we'll shake up a recipe for you!
  2. By Ingredient and Category: You can find any cocktail that is a particular type of drink and has your favourite ingredient!
  3. Random: Feeling a bit overwhelmed by the possibilities? Hit the random button!

Can I save a recipe for later?

Yes! press the little heart next to a cocktail to add it to your favourites bar. You can store up to 5 cocktails.
